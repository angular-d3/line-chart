Smart

n3-charts adapts itself to your data. Tooltips are never cropped, data is never hidden. Don't worry about margins, sizes and other stuff. Just tell n3-charts how you want to see your data : lines, columns, areas, colors... n3-charts lets you define what matters and handles the rest.

Interactive

Hide and show your series, hover the points to display a cute tooltip. n3-charts is data-centered, but that doesn't mean it has to be boring ! We want n3-charts interactivity to be discrete and awesome.

Beautiful

Powered by D3.js, n3-charts renders your data in a simple, crispy way. Charts are all about visualizing data, right ? So let's make it beautiful, accurate and easy-to-integrate in your web pages.
